Lasagna

Crispy lasagna perfect for any family dinner or occasion

This recipe has been my go to for creating nice dinner for the family. It always turns out cripsy on the outside and perfectly cooked on the inside. Easy to make AND easy to store, it is truly on of the best recipes you can follow.

Ingredients

Cheese filling

  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cups Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • 3/4 pound ground beef
  • 3/4 pound ground italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40 ounces marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Lasagna

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese
